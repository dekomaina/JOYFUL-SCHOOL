About Our School

Excellence in Education Since 2005

Located in the heart of Nairobi along Ngong Road, Joyful Stars Academy has been dedicated to providing exceptional education to Kenyan students from Pre-Primary through Form 4. Our experienced faculty, innovative curriculum aligned with KICD requirements, and supportive community create an environment where every student can thrive.

Our campus features modern facilities including science labs equipped for KCSE practicals, computer centers with internet access, art studios showcasing Kenyan talent, and athletic fields where our teams compete in the Nairobi County Sports Association tournaments.

At Joyful Stars Academy, we celebrate our African heritage through cultural days, Swahili language programs, and traditional music and dance, while preparing students to excel in national exams and beyond.

Our Programs

Primary Education (CBC)

Grades 1-6: Our Competency-Based Curriculum program focuses on building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and digital literacy through engaging, culturally relevant learning experiences aligned with Kenyan education standards.

KICD Approved

Secondary Education

Form 1-4: As students prepare for KCSE, our program emphasizes critical thinking, STEM education, and leadership development with a focus on African innovation and problem-solving relevant to Kenya's development goals.

KCSE Excellence

Extracurricular

From traditional Kenyan dance (Isukuti, Benga) and music to football, netball, and coding clubs, our extracurricular programs celebrate African culture while developing 21st-century skills and talents.
